Grilled Cheese Sandwiches
with Bacon and Tomato

list Of   Ingredients:

  •   Bacon
  •   Jalopino Peppers
  •   Tomato
  •   Cheddar Cheese
  •   BUtter
  •   Cream Cheese
  •   Baguette bread
  •   Salt

Instruction:

  •   To prepare this delicious sandwich, do the following steps in order:
  •   First, we need to grill the jalapeno peppers. For this purpose, it is better to put the peppers on the heat for about five minutes until they are half burnt around them.
  •   Now we put the peppers in the foil and after five minutes we take them out of the foil, remove their skin and cut them in half and throw away the seeds of the peppers.
  •   We put the meat bacon in the pan and put it on the heat. After about eight minutes,
    we take them off the heat.
  •   Put the butter in a pan and put it on the heat until it melts and put the bread in it for two minutes until it is slightly toasted.
  •   We put cheddar cheese, fried bacon, grilled peppers and tomatoes on a bun.
  •   We put cheddar cheese and cream cheese on the other bread.
  •   We put the bread on top of each other and fry the sandwich in butter.
  •   The End
